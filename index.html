At a glance

Discoveri is a calm companion for a year of self‑discovery. One meaningful question a day; threaded follow‑ups; narrative summaries every 90 days. Everything is yours to keep, private by default, with one‑tap export or delete. Streaks are gentle and pauseable; progress is recognized across Focus, Connection, Movement, and Learning.

Feature plan

  • Adaptive prompts — context‑aware callbacks to prior answers.
  • Threaded reflections — back‑and‑forth dialog that compounds.
  • Privacy‑first — local by default; one‑tap export & delete.
  • Gentle streaks — pause/backfill without harsh penalties.
  • Values map — cross‑entry insights & weekly highlights.
  • Trimester reports — narrative recaps every ~90 days.

Pro (premium)

  • Deep Insights+ — higher‑context model, longer threads.
  • Audio prompts — serene voice & soundscapes.
  • Guided routines — short, optional micro‑exercises.
  • Theme packs — monthly libraries & creator packs.
  • Mood overlays — optional trend layer over insights.
  • Goals view — turn reflections into gentle goals.
  • Knowledge threads — connect ideas across months.
  • Relationship prompts — paired reflections.
  • Energy rituals — movement micro‑breaks.
  • Supporter badge — early labs & priority roadmap.
